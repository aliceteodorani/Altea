Urbino e le Città del Libro

2022 and 2023 Editions
Supervised by Marco Tortoioli Ricci
Illustrations by Mara Cerri

Identity / Editorial design

"Urbino e le Città del Libro" (Urbino and the Book Cities) is a festival born in 2014 with the pourpose of involving in a single event the book excellences of the city of Urbino and its territory. The festival aims to promote reading and publishing culture through presentations, readings, meetings with authors and publishers, round tables, workshops and laboratories, concerts, exhibitions and twinning with Italian and international "book cities."
