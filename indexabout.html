Ciao! I'm Alice (2000, Cesena, IT) an Italian graphic designer currently specializing in editorial design at ISIA Urbino. I would describe myself as outgoing and curious, always eager to take on new challenges. I thrive in collaborative environments and consider myself a hard worker with a strong desire to continuously learn and grow. My experience in the scouts has been a formative part of my life, teaching me valuable teamwork skills and fostering independence. Some of my interests include analog photography, cinema, and music.
