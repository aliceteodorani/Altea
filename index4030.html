2022/2023
Supervised by: Joost Grootens
ISIA U

Editorial / Information design
Encyclopedia
w/ Roberta Antinolfi

In today's digital landscape, the democratization of design has revolutionized the creative process, enabling individuals without advanced skills to engage in design practices. "The User as Designer" is an encyclopedia that explores this phenomenon, shedding light on the myriad software, tools, and resources that empower users to unleash their creative potential.
