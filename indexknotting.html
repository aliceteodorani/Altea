Knotting the space – 20 ropes, 15 beams

2023
Supervised by: Armand Mevis
ISIA U

Editorial design
Poster and booklet

70x100 poster on one side, folded in an eight pages booklet. Six modular structures made with a maximum of 20 ropes and 15 beams each. The purpose is to challenge and rethink the traditional ways in which we can use and occupy public spaces. The rope is a powerful tool, it’s versatility allows to create flexible designs adjustable to suit different needs. Knotted structures can be used to create inclusive gathering areas, modify the space and be part of it.
