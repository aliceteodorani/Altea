Erberto Carboni
fra grafica e pubblicità

2022/2023 – ISIA U
Supervised by Leonardo Sonnoli
and Irene Bacchi

Editorial design / Research
w/ Maria Giulia Leonardi
and Luca Mocellin

With an introductory text by Carlo Vinti entitled "Italian Graphics from 1945 to Today" this book is a collection of textual and iconographic materials documenting the work of Italian graphic designer Erberto Carboni throughout his long career. Erberto Carboni, a celebrated and multifaceted figure as in Italian graphic design, is portrayed through the words of colleagues, critics, historians, and family members. The volume presents itself as an anthological archive that aims to collect and showcase over time the stylistic evolution of the graphic designer from Parma, demonstrating his versatility and the renewed freshness in devising consistently diverse solutions.
